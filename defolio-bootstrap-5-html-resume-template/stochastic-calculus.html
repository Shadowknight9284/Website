Stochastic Calculus Application in Finance

May 2024 - August 2024

Project Overview

A research project exploring the mathematical foundations of financial models through stochastic calculus, focusing on the Black-Scholes model and its underlying assumptions.

Research Areas

  • Martingales and their role in financial mathematics
  • Brownian motion applications in option pricing
  • Black-Scholes model derivation and limitations
  • Stochastic differential equations in finance

Key Findings

  • Analysis of market efficiency assumptions
  • Impact of volatility on option pricing
  • Limitations of continuous-time models
  • Alternative approaches to option valuation

Tools & Methods

MATLAB R LaTeX Python Statistical Analysis
